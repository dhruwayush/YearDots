Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 20, 2026

Thank you for choosing YearDots. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). We rely on a simple principle: your data is yours.

1. Data Collection

YearDots is designed to be privacy-first. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data, usage statistics, or location information. The App functions entirely offline.

2. Local Storage

All settings and preferences (such as theme colors, grid configuration, and display options) are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your phone.

3. Permissions

The App may request the following permissions to function correctly:

  • Set Wallpaper: Required to set the live wallpaper on your home screen.
  • Battery/Background Optimization: Required to allow the live wallpaper to run efficiently without being killed by the system.

4. Third-Party Services

YearDots contains no third-party analytics, tracking SDKs, or advertising networks.
